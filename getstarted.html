WIN 10 INSTALLATION

Step 1. Installation

- Double-click serato dj pro 2.3.1.exe after downloading,

- Agree to the T&C's then click install.

- Click yes to proceed.

Serato DJ Pro will now perform a standard installation 

Step 2. Proceed

- When prompted click cancel to Quicktime player installation then click yes.

- Finally click Finish.

- Click and open Serato DJ Pro from your desktop or start menu

- Click X to close 'whats new in 2.3.1' screen 

Step 3. Done

- Lets Start Djing
